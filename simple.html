HipHop Adhi HipHop Adhi is an Indian rapper, music composer, and actor known for pioneering Tamil hip-hop music and hits like “Club le Mabbu le” and his film Meesaya Murukku.

HipHop Adhi, born on February 20, 1990, in Sathyamangalam, Tamil Nadu, started exploring music in his school days and became famous through his independent hip-hop releases on YouTube, eventually co-founding "HipHop Tamizha." 		Some of HipHop Adhi’s famous songs include “Club le Mabbu le,” “Vaadi Pulla Vaadi,” “Thani Oruvan Theme,” and “Sodakku” from Thaanaa Serndha Koottam.

Vijay Antony Vijay Antony is an Indian actor, music composer, and producer known for his impactful roles and music in Tamil films like Naan, Bichagadu, and Salim.

Vijay Antony was born on July 24, 1975, in Nagercoil, Tamil Nadu. He developed an early interest in music and started learning to play the keyboard. After completing his education, he moved to Chennai to pursue a career in music and initially worked as a music programmer before gaining recognition as a composer and later transitioning into acting.		 One of Vijay Antony's famous songs is “Naan Pizhaippeno” from the film Naan (2012), which he also composed and sung. The song became immensely popular for its soulful melody.
