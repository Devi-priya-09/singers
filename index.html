GV Prakash G.V. Prakash Kumar is an Indian music composer, playback singer, and actor known for his work in Tamil cinema. "Yathe Yathe" from the movie *Aadukalam*
Anirudh Ravichander Anirudh Ravichander is a celebrated Indian composer and singer known for his modern, catchy soundtracks and viral hits. One of Anirudh's most famous songs is "Why This Kolaveri Di" from the film 3, which became a global sensation.
HipHop Adhi HipHop Adhi is an Indian rapper, music composer, and actor known for pioneering Tamil hip-hop music and hits like “Club le Mabbu le” and his film Meesaya Murukku. Some of HipHop Adhi’s famous songs include “Club le Mabbu le,” “Vaadi Pulla Vaadi,” “Thani Oruvan Theme,” and “Sodakku” from Thaanaa Serndha Koottam.
Sid Sriram Sid Sriram is an Indian-American playback singer and music composer known for his soulful voice and hit songs in Tamil, Telugu, and Hindi cinema, with popular tracks like “Thalli Pogathey” and “Vaan Varuvan.” “Thalli Pogathey” from Achcham Yenbadhu Madamaiyada (2016)
Vijay Antony Vijay Antony is an Indian actor, music composer, and producer known for his impactful roles and music in Tamil films like Naan, Bichagadu, and Salim. One of Vijay Antony's famous songs is “Naan Pizhaippeno” from the film Naan (2012), which he also composed and sung. The song became immensely popular for its soulful melody.
